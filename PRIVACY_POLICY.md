# Privacy Policy for AX Player

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

AX Player ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Video Library Access**
- We access your device's video files to display them in the app
- Video metadata (title, duration, size, resolution)
- Playback history and preferences
- Bookmarks and playlists you create

**User Preferences**
- App settings and configurations
- Theme and display preferences
- Subtitle customization settings
- Gesture and control preferences

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**
- App usage statistics
- Feature usage analytics
- Playback duration and frequency
- Error logs and crash reports (with your consent)

**Device Information**
- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version
- App version
- Screen resolution and device capabilities

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect your personal identity information (name, email, phone)
- We do NOT access your contacts or messages
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your camera or microphone
- We do NOT collect payment information (if applicable, handled by Google Play)

## How We Use Your Information

### Video Management
- Display your local video files in the library
- Generate thumbnails for video preview
- Save playback position for resume functionality
- Organize videos into playlists and folders

### App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- Optimize performance and user experience
- Develop new features based on usage trends

### Personalization
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Provide customized recommendations (optional)
- Sync settings across devices (if opted in)

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All video files remain on your device
- Playlists and bookmarks stored locally in encrypted database
- Settings stored securely using Android DataStore
- No video content is uploaded to our servers

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- If you opt into cloud backup:
  - Settings and preferences may be synced
  - Playlists and bookmarks may be backed up
  - Video files are NEVER uploaded

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)
**Purpose**: Analytics, crash reporting, performance monitoring

**Data Shared**:
- Anonymous usage statistics
- Crash logs (no personal information)
- Performance metrics

**Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

**Your Control**: You can opt out in Settings → Privacy → Analytics

### Coil Image Loading
**Purpose**: Efficient image and thumbnail loading

**Data**: Only processes local files, no external data sent

### Google Play Services
**Purpose**: App distribution and updates

**Data**: Handled by Google Play Store policies

## Data Retention

- **Playback History**: Retained until you clear it or uninstall the app
- **Preferences**: Retained until you reset or uninstall
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for 14 months, then automatically deleted
- **Crash Logs**: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Data**: Access all your data in Settings → About → Data Management
- **Clear History**: Clear watch history anytime
- **Clear Bookmarks**: Delete individual or all bookmarks
- **Reset Settings**: Reset to default settings
- **Delete Account Data**: Uninstall app to remove all local data

### Analytics and Tracking
- **Opt Out**: Disable analytics in Settings → Privacy → Analytics
- **Crash Reporting**: Disable in Settings → Privacy → Crash Reporting
- **Personalized Ads**: We don't show personalized ads

### Data Portability
- **Export Settings**: Export your settings to a file
- **Export Playlists**: Export playlists to M3U format
- **Import Data**: Import from exported files

## Children's Privacy

AX Player is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

## Security

### Data Protection Measures
- **Encryption**: All preferences encrypted using AndroidKeyStore
- **Secure Storage**: Scoped storage compliance for Android 10+
- **HTTPS Only**: All network communication uses HTTPS
- **Code Obfuscation**: Release builds use R8 obfuscation
- **No Sensitive Logging**: No personal data in logs

### Your Responsibility
- Keep your device secure with screen lock
- Don't root your device (compromises security)
- Keep the app updated for latest security patches

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notification for major changes

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

### Your GDPR Rights
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to our processing of your data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent anytime

### Legal Basis for Processing
- **Consent**: You explicitly opt in to analytics and crash reporting
- **Legitimate Interest**: Necessary functionality like playback and library management
- **Contract**: If you purchase premium features

### Data Protection Officer
For GDPR inquiries, contact: dpo@axplayer.com

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

### Your CCPA Rights
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### How to Exercise Rights
- Email: privacy@axplayer.com
- In-App: Settings → Privacy → Request Data Deletion
